𓋹 THE PROPHECY
In the age before memory, the high priests of the Order of Ra encoded their most sacred knowledge — not in stone, not in papyrus — but in sound itself.
The vibrations of the temple walls hold a message.
But the message is not for the unworthy.
It cannot be heard.
It cannot be seen.
It can only be summoned — by those who possess the priests' own tools.
